                                                                  [B][COLOR deepskyblue]Recent updates to the wizard:[/COLOR][/B]
• [B]Clean cache/packages/thumbs[/B] added to the maintenance menu (inspired by ez maintenance+).
• [B]Optimize Database[/B] added to the maintenance menu (inspired by OptiClean and recommended by TheFather999). This will help to keep databases compact and Kodi snappy over time.
• [B]Force Update Repos[/B] added to the maintenance menu. Does much of what it says. It will force an update of all installed repositories.
• [B]Authorize[/B] function inside the wizard that will show installed addons and then services you can authorize with them from the sub-menus. [See Authorize Debrid/Trakt in build wizard]
• [B]AutoClean[/B] feature that can be turned on in settings and set to run every X amount of days [ON by default].
• [B]Saved Data[/B] will now retain all Trakt and Debrid data for all included addons in my builds upon build updates. This is provided you have selected Debrid/Trakt in the saved data settings of the wizard (accessed via settings or upon first launch of wizard).
